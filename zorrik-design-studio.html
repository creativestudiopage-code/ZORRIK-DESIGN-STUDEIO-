The Zorrik Edge

Functionality Meets Superior Craft

A false ceiling does more than hold lights. It conceals electrical wiring, improves acoustics, and insulates your rooms against heat. Paired with high-grade flooring materials, it redefines how your space feels underfoot and overhead.

  • check_circle
    Integrated Lighting Curation Seamless mapping of magnetic track lights, spot cylinders, and indirect LED strips matching your furniture placement.
  • check_circle
    Precision Alignment & Leveling We use laser levels and heavy-gauge galvanized framing sections to ensure zero ceiling sag or floor misalignment.
  • check_circle
    Superior Acoustic Insulation Optional glass wool insulation inside the false ceiling framing to reduce rooftop heat and sound transmission.
